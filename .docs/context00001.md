# 🌿 LeafCloud Capstone Project: Experiment Setup Guide

### 1. The Materials
*   **Nutrients:** NutriHydro Lettuce Formula (Set of "Grow" & "Bloom").
    *   *Note:* You also have Iron Chelate (to prevent yellowing) and pH Up.
    *   *Capacity:* The 100ml bottles are concentrates and are **sufficient** for the entire experiment (can make 30-50 Liters).
*   **Containers:**
    *   **Preferred:** Opaque Stackable Bins (approx. 17.8L max volume). These block light (preventing algae) and have enough water volume so you don't need to refill often.
    *   **Alternative:** Clear Plastic Cylinders. *Critical Fix:* Must be wrapped in foil/duct tape to block light.
*   **Measurement Tool:**
    *   **Requirement:** A **3ml or 5ml Plastic Syringe** (no needle).
    *   *Why?* The measuring cup is too inaccurate for the small amounts needed for the 25% and 50% setups.

### 2. Nutrient Mixing "Cheat Sheet"
**Preparation Rule:** Never mix "Grow" and "Bloom" directly together.
**Correct Order:** Water First ➔ Add "Grow" ➔ Stir ➔ Add "Bloom" ➔ Stir.

| Experiment Level | Dosage (Per 1 Liter of Water) | Expected Visual Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **125% (Over)** | **3.1 ml** Grow + **3.1 ml** Bloom | Toxicity, "Tip Burn" (brown edges), dark green. |
| **100% (Control)** | **2.5 ml** Grow + **2.5 ml** Bloom | Healthy, standard growth. |
| **50% (Deficiency)** | **1.25 ml** Grow + **1.25 ml** Bloom | Smaller size, lighter green leaves. |
| **25% (Severe)** | **0.6 ml** Grow + **0.6 ml** Bloom | Severe stunting, pale yellow (chlorotic). |

### 3. Procedure & Rules
*   **Seedling Transfer:** Do **not** transfer seedlings until **roots are visible** sticking out of the net pot/foam. If they are hidden, wait a few more days.
*   **Water Level (Kratky Method):**
    *   The water should **touch the roots**.
    *   Do **not** submerge the entire plant or cup. The plant stems need air to breathe.
*   **Refilling (If needed):** If water runs low, pre-mix the nutrient solution in a separate pitcher using the **exact same concentration ratio** before pouring it into the bin. Do not just add water.
